Inio Asano

"The voice of his generation" - Yomiuri Shimbun

  • September 22, 1980 - Asano Inio,born in Ishioka, Ibaraki Prefecture, Japan.
  • Japanese manga author (mangaka), known for creating character-driven, realist stories, ranging from slice of life to psychological horror.
  • 2001 - won his first prize, GX competition for young manga artists.
Notable Works:
